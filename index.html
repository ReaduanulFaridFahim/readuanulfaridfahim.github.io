About

I'm a Computer Science & Engineering graduate from North South University, specialized in Machine Learning and Artificial Intelligence, with a genuine pull toward data analytics, research, and software engineering. That technical foundation — Python, HTML/CSS/JS, and a growing portfolio of ML and computer-vision projects — is the lens I bring to every problem I touch.

I put that same discipline to work every day as an Airport Operations Executive at US-Bangla Airlines, coordinating ramp and ground activity where safety procedures and split-second timing matter as much as any algorithm. Structured systems, whether they're running on data or on a runway, are where I do my best work.

And I've been a sports person for as long as I've been anything else. As Joint Secretary of the North South University Games & Sports Club, I've spent four years organizing tournaments and building a community around the game — because for me, sport isn't a side interest, it's a lifelong constant, right alongside code and aircraft.
